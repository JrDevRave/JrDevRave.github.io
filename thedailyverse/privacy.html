Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Daily Verse's automated content publishing pipeline (the "Service") handles information. The Service is a personal, single-operator automation tool with no public user accounts.

1. Information We Collect

The Service stores OAuth access and refresh tokens for the operator's own YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram/Facebook accounts, used solely to publish content. No information is collected from third parties or platform end users.

2. How We Use Information

Stored credentials are used exclusively to authenticate API requests that publish video content to the operator's own connected accounts on a scheduled basis.

3. Data Sharing

No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Credentials are used only for direct API communication with YouTube, TikTok, and Meta (Instagram/Facebook) on the operator's behalf.

4. Data Retention

Credentials and publishing records are retained locally for as long as the Service remains active, and deleted if the Service is discontinued or a platform integration is revoked.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service does not knowingly collect information from children. It has no public sign-up or account creation flow of any kind.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Policy at any time. Continued operation of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
